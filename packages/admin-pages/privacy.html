Our Core Privacy Commitment

xSwarm collects exactly two types of information:

  1. Account Information: Email/phone for login and payment processing via Stripe
  2. Website Analytics: General usage patterns (pages visited, referrers, etc.)

That's it. We don't collect communication content, device fingerprints, location data, or browsing history beyond our site.

By using xSwarm services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

Your Communication Privacy Guarantee

We do NOT collect, store, monitor, or analyze ANY communication content between you and Boss AI.

  • Zero Message Content: Your SMS, voice, and email content is never stored
  • Zero Voice Recordings: Calls are processed in real-time only, never recorded
  • Zero Conversation History: No transcripts, no logs, no archives
  • Real-Time Processing Only: Content is immediately discarded after AI response

Your conversations are private. Period.
