# Animation Details

Each state has multiple frames that cycle to create smooth animation:

- Idle: 2 frames alternating notes (500ms each)
- Listening: 4 frames with rising/falling bars (200ms each)
- Speaking: Variable frames based on audio level (80-300ms)
- Processing: 4 frames with moving dots (200ms each)
- AI Speaking: 4 frames with sound waves (150ms each)

# Color Scheme

- Idle: Gray border, white text
- Listening: Cyan border and highlights
- Speaking: Yellow border and highlights
- Processing: Magenta border and highlights
- AI Speaking: Green border and highlights
