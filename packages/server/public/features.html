Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?
For voice minutes and SMS messages, once you reach your monthly limit, those features will be temporarily disabled until the next billing cycle. Email communication continues uninterrupted. You can upgrade your plan at any time to get higher limits immediately.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade your plan instantly and start using the new features immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. We'll prorate any changes to ensure you only pay for what you use.
What is xSwarm Buzz marketing platform?
xSwarm Buzz is our integrated marketing automation platform included with Project Manager and CTO tiers. It helps you manage customer communications, automate marketing campaigns, and track engagement across email, SMS, and voice channels - all powered by your AI assistant.
Do you really not store any communication data?
Correct! We have a strict zero-retention policy for all voice calls, SMS messages, and emails. Your communication data is processed in real-time and never stored on our servers. We only retain account information (payment details, credentials) and basic usage metrics for billing purposes. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
What kind of support comes with each plan?
AI Buddy (Free): Community forum support
AI Secretary: Priority email support with 48-hour response time
AI Project Manager: Priority email support with 24-hour response time
AI CTO: Phone support with dedicated account manager and immediate response
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
You can start with our free AI Buddy plan to test the platform. For paid tiers, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your AI Secretary or Project Manager subscription in the first two weeks, we'll refund your payment in full.
What's included in Enterprise custom pricing?
Enterprise pricing is tailored to your specific needs and includes unlimited usage, dedicated infrastructure, custom integrations, SLA guarantees, white-label options, and dedicated support. Contact our sales team at enterprise@xswarm.ai for a custom quote.
